Release Info

This is the second public release of the ODI exposure
time calculator; it has only been tested yet with a
small user basis. However, the throughput model has
been adjusted to match the photometric
zeropoints that have been measured in February 2014 after
thorough mirror cleaning.

Use at your own risk!
